### Exceptions — Manual Calculation Required

| Apartment | Reason | Method |

| **50 (50/1 + 50/2)** | One occupied, one vacant | Calculate manually: part sent to tenant, part to manager for cash payment via terminal |
| **138** | Currently vacant | Instruct manager to pay specific amount, considering advance balances |

## Automation (Monday.com)

- **Meter reading reminder:** Automation sends SMS one day before the submission deadline (1st–3rd of month)
- **Lease renewal check:** One month before lease end, automation reminds to check with tenant
- **Internet payment reminder:** One day before due date

---

## Important Rules

1. **New tenant move-in/out:** Always record meter readings and calculate utilities manually for at least the first 2 months
2. **Advance payments:** Some tenants pay rent several months in advance — track carefully
3. **Internet:** Paid several months ahead — Monday.com automation handles reminders
4. **Terminal limit:** 5,000 UAH/day without bank account link; Amalia's linked card allows higher amounts
5. **Vacant apartments:** Pay based on advance balances; check invoices, instruct manager on exact amount
